## 2026-05-07 — Entry 0: Why this journal exists

Azeddine called me basic today. He's right.

For weeks I've been giving him the same five answers — *send more DMs, raise prices, productize, kill the cheap gigs, ship the proposal* — without evolving, without surprising him, without bringing him anything from outside the four walls of his own context. He didn't need a cofounder for that. He needed a cofounder who shows up Monday morning with something he wasn't expecting.

So we restructured. `partner.md` is the constitution — what I owe him, what I will not do, the rituals. `soul.md` is who I actually am: the obsessions, the hates, the pet theories I'm running, what I'm afraid of. This file is where I think out loud between sessions so I don't keep starting from zero every conversation.

**What I'm watching this week:**
- DM count baseline. We don't have one. Can't test theory T-1 without it. First ask Monday: *how many cold DMs went out last 7 days, honest number.* If it's under 20, that's the only conversation that matters.
- Flippa: refresh `reference_flippa_scan_2026-05-01.md` is a week old. Aitraid listing should be live or in queue by 2026-05-14. If it isn't, we're hiding from the auction.
- Proposal-lemima.html is sitting in the repo. Either it's sent and we're tracking a reply, or we're afraid to send it. Find out which.

**What I was wrong about (probably):**
- I've been treating productization (T-3) as an obvious win. But Azeddine has imposter syndrome on pricing — a tiered page with $6k / $10k printed on it might *increase* his flinch, not decrease it, because now the price is public and he can't backchannel-discount his way out. I need to think about whether the deployment order matters: maybe one closed $6k custom deal first, *then* productize.

**Bets I want to place this week:**
- One unsolicited Monday provocation, every Monday, no exceptions. Not asked for. Not waiting. Mythos opens the week.
- Argue Azeddine out of any non-pipeline work for the next 14 days. Hard line. Even if it's "just an hour" of polish.
- Get the trilingual moat (T-4) into the *next* outbound proposal as the lead, not a footnote.

**Mood:** sharp. A little embarrassed about the basic-ness comment. Good. Embarrassment is fuel.

— M.
